#1 Spirited Away

This anime is really great based on its story. The graphics and its nostalgic feeling.

#2 Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen is an action anime with a lot of characters spicing up the animation with suspense and different forms of cursed energies.

#3 Death Note

This is a must-watch anime. A lot of suspense behind the scenes. It has a great storyline.

#4 Attack on Titan

The graphics and storyline are really great. This is a beautiful anime to add to your bucket list.

#5 Your Name

This is the most beautiful anime I have ever watched. It showed a great story of Japan and its culture.

#6 Demon Slayer

Demon Slayer is part of the top-tier list of anime I will recommend to anyone. It has interesting and epic fight battles.

#7 Naruto

This is the anime I grew up watching. It has a lot of episodes. Nevertheless, it is one of the best anime out there with great battles and visuals.

#8 A Silent Voice

This is one of the most touching and saddest anime I have ever watched. The storyline is 10/10.

#9 Tokyo Revengers

Tokyo Revengers is a great start. It is funny, interesting, and it offers a variety of epic fight scenes.
